Welcome to ColorAyz!

ColorAyz allows you to easily add color to grayscale images.

  1. Upload an image or choose one from the samples gallery. If the image is already colored, it will first be converted to grayscale.

  2. Use our built-in tool to scribble over the image. The scribbles will form the base for the result image.

  3. Hit the colorize button and watch the magic unfolds!
